How to Install

Stock Devices

 1. Use your PC or Mac to open this page and click the button with "Github Repo".
2. Go to release page and download the newest version.
3. Use Cydia Impactor or other tools to sideload it to your device.
4. Open Settings in your iDevice, click General-Profiles and trust the option with your Apple ID.
If you use free Apple ID to sign the App, you will need to resign it every week.

Jailbroken Devices


1. Add my Cydia Repo by clicking "Add Cydia Repo".
2. Open Cydia and install iKanColleCommand.
3. (Optional) If App not showing up after install, run uicache.
No resign needed, but App will not work without jailbreak activated.
